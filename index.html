About

Full-Stack Developer · Tallinn, Estonia · raunoot@gmail.com

I'm a developer who is passionate about UI/UX that can be described as well thought-out and fluid minimalism.
Whenever I'm writing code, I'm also thinking about how to make the end-user experience as fast, beautiful and seamless as possible. In todays age of full-page banner ads and autoplay videos, I aim to provide visitors with a breath of fresh air, whilst not compromising on functionality nor style.

Other than spending my time coding, I enjoy being in the outdoors when possible and creating music with my friends.
I also play guitar in a rock band named Loose Grace and we are expecting to release an EP in late 2020.

When forced indoors (estonian winter can be long), I'm a big fan of sci-fi and detective shows like Stargate & True Detective and enjoy an occasional game of RTS from the Age of Empires or Anno series. I'm also, obviously, a big tech nerd and like to keep up with all the latest trends in the industry.
